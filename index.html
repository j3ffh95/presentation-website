⚽️ Soccer Facts ⚽️

In many parts of the world soccer is known as football, or even American football. It is the most popular sport in the world, being played in more than 200 countries. Approximately 250 million people play soccer around the world every year, and it has been an Olympic sport since 1900.

Fact #2

In the early 1800s soccer was invented in Newgate Prison in London by prisoners who had been punished for stealing. Their punishment had been to have their hands cut off.

Fact #3

The USSA was the first American professional soccer league. They played from 1919 for two years. They were paid $0.35 every time they scored.

Fact #4

Soccer fields are called pitches. Pitch and slope have the same meaning in soccer. If the field is a regulation field, it slopes 5 degrees up, from one end of the field to the other. Teams switch goal nets during the game so they both have the same uphill slope for half the game.
